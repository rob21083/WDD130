Content

Home page

The purpose of my website will be to trademark my skills as a programmer and what I can contribute to an employer or an individual considering me for potential hire or work. The first page of my website will be entitled Bio. The Bio will be a page consisting of personal information about me, where I live, hobbies I like to participate in, why I would be a good fit for potential work force or employer. The page will have an image of me another image of something I like. It will be creatively styled and geared toward displaying a good face. This will be the main page when you go to the web site and will have a navigation bar at the top with two other pages you can go see. The text for the bio will read something like this: "Hey, I’m David! Welcome to my site, this serves as a place to see my coding experience and what I have been able to accomplish. I love coding, its a way to be creative and share unique ways to excite the mind. I got started into coding recently and am anxious to continue growing my skills and developing better ways to code. I am from a small town in Nevada, Winnemucca. I am now currently living in Utah and am happlie married for just over a year. Feel free to check the site out and let me know what you think!"

Resume

The next page will contain my resume, it will be names resume. Here you will be able to see my resume and past experiences that would contribute in a work space. This Page will have an Image of my resume to see while viewing the page. This page will be a see what skills I have while to next page will be a demonstration of the skills I have. It will also have contact information to reach out to me if they are interested in talking to my via phone or email. This page will be centered around the resume so not the most flattering but will need good styling to make it appealing to see the resume. Text from resume will go as follows: "Here is my resume, detailed below is a list of experiences that have helped move my career forward."
